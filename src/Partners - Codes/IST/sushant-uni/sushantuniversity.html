Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is the Intellipaat School of Technology (IST)?

IST is an industry-driven four-year undergraduate technology program that prepares students to become top software engineers and innovators. It blends academic learning with real-world tech skills, mentorship from industry experts and IIT faculty, global exposure, and immersive learning visits.

What make IST stand out from others?

Unlike conventional programs that focus on theory, IST emphasizes industry-designed curricula, hands-on projects, product building, global immersion, entrepreneurship, internships, and placement support.

Students will learn from Industry Experts, SME’s. Master classes from IIT faculty, top founders and others. From day 1 students start working on building real world products with real impact.

What degree will I earn upon completing IST?

Students receive a B.Tech degree from a UGC-recognized university partner upon program completion. Alongside the degree, participants also earn industry ready certifications from IIT Patna & Microsoft.

Is this a full-time program?

Yes, this is an on-campus program at the university campus. There is no option for correspondence or online studies.

Admissions & Eligibility

What are the education eligibility requirements to join IST?

You must have completed Class 12th with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics) from a recognized board, with a minimum of 55% marks in Mathematic.

What is the admission process?

The typical steps are:

  • Submit your application
  • Appear for the IST Entrance Exam
  • Participate in the personal interview
  • Receive the offer letter
  • Confirm your seat and enroll

IST Entrance Exam

What is the IST Entrance Exam?

The IST Entrance Exam is an aptitude- and potential-based assessment designed to evaluate a student’s logical thinking, problem-solving ability, and interest in technology.

Is clearing JEE required to appear for the IST?

No. JEE is not required. We follow our own entrance process to identify students with strong potential for tech careers.

What is the exam pattern?

The exam typically includes questions on:

  • Logical reasoning
  • Basic mathematics
  • Analytical thinking
  • Technology awareness (No advanced coding knowledge is required.)

Is there any negative marking?

No, there is no negative marking, allowing students to attempt all questions confidently.

How is the exam conducted?

The IST Entrance Exam is conducted online, allowing students to take it from the comfort of their home.

What happens after clearing the IST Entrance Exam?

Students who clear the IST Entrance Exam move on to a personal interview round. This interaction helps us understand the student’s personality, motivation, communication skills, and interest in technology—beyond just academic performance.

Does the IST Entrance Exam offer scholarships?

Yes. Merit-based scholarships are offered based on entrance exam performance and overall profile.

How can students prepare for the IST Entrance Exam?

Students can prepare by:

  • Practicing basic math and logical reasoning
  • Strengthening analytical thinking
  • Reviewing sample questions shared by IST

Projects

Are the projects industry-aligned?

Yes. Projects at IST are industry-aligned and reflect real challenges from the tech industry. Students get access to 50+ projects across software development, full-stack applications, and machine learning, enabling them to gain hands-on problem-solving experience and showcase practical, real-world expertise to future employers through strong portfolios.

Apart from projects, will I be building real world products to solve real world problems?

At IST, students are encouraged to work on real-world, hands-on projects starting from the first semester. They identify real problems, build innovative solutions, and implement them using modern technologies. By graduation, every student is expected to build at least two end-to-end products, developing an entrepreneurial mindset, strong problem-solving skills, and a work-ready portfolio—preparing them to become innovators and job creators, not just job seekers.

Do these projects & products help with internships and jobs?

Yes—projects give students work-ready experience that can be showcased in interviews, during internships, and when applying for jobs, helping you stand out to recruiters.

Will IST help with internships and job placements?

Yes. IST offers 12 months of internship opportunities with top MNCs and startups, plus strong placement support. Students benefit from resume building, interview preparation, GitHub and LinkedIn profiling, and access to a network of 3100+ hiring partners.

What kind of career outcomes have Intellipaat alumni achieved?

Extra Learning & Entrepreneurship

What are “Global Immersion” and immersive learning visits?

IST organizes international tech immersion programs and tech conclaves visits. These experiences expose students to world-class technologies, global best practices, and cutting-edge innovation ecosystems. Students also get the opportunity to interact with some of the brightest minds across the world, helping them broaden their perspective and build a global mindset.

What is the Innovation & Entrepreneurship Hub?

IST has an Innovation hub where students can develop startup ideas, learn how to pitch to investors, and receive mentorship for entrepreneurial journeys. A dedicated fund of Rs. 10 crore has been allocated for providing support to promising ideas.

Can IST prepare me for higher studies or abroad?

Yes — IST equips students for both career roles in tech and further education, including pathways for global MS programs through study-abroad opportunities.

Is IST suitable for students who want to start a business?

Absolutely. With entrepreneurship mentorship and a dedicated innovation hub, students can pursue startup ideas and even secure seed funding guidance.

Where are IST campuses located?
