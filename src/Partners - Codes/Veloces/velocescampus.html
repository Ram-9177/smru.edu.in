What is Veloces Campus? +
Veloces Campus is an EdTech organisation that offers Industry - Academia integrated B.Tech and B.Sc programs designed in collaboration with Microsoft. These programs are delivered at top UGC/AICTE approved universities across India. Veloces is a Microsoft Gold Partner, bringing industry-relevant technology education to students.
What makes Veloces Campus's program special? +
Veloces programs are co-designed with Microsoft, integrating AI, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, AR/VR, IoT, and Blockchain from day one. Students also earn global certifications from Microsoft, AWS, Google Cloud, Red Hat and Certiport alongside their degree.
Where is the Veloces Campus program offered? +
Veloces Campus programs are currently offered at 5 university campuses — Ajeenkya DY Patil University (Pune), Periyar Maniammai Institute of Science & Technology (Thanjavur), Sandip University (Nashik), Stmarys University (Hyderabad), and Vidhyadeep University (Surat).
Is the B.Tech degree awarded by Veloces Campus? +
No. Veloces Campus is not a university. The B.Tech degree is awarded by the respective UGC-recognized and AICTE-approved university.
Is the B.Tech degree valid for jobs and higher studies in India and abroad? +
Yes. The B.Tech degree is awarded by UGC-recognized and AICTE-approved universities, making it fully valid for jobs, higher studies, and further education in India and abroad.
Does Veloces Campus provide Management programs +
Yes. Currently Veloces Campus offers BBA Aviation Management in two of its partner university campuses (Sandip University - Nashik, Stmarys University - Hyderabad).
Who is eligible to apply at Veloces Campus? +
B.Tech - Students who have passed Higher Secondary (HSC) or equivalent from a recognized board with 50% and above in Physics, Chemistry and Maths (PCM) are eligible to apply.
BBA Aviation Management - Passed 10+2 or equivalent course in any discipline from recognised Board/ Council/ University with minimum 45% marks.
What is the admission process? +
The admission process has 6 steps: (1) Fill the application form online, (2) Take the VSAT exam, (3) Personal Interview, (4) Counselling session, (5) Book your seat, (6) Document verification.
Visit admissions.velocescampus.com to get started.
How do I apply? +
You can apply online at admissions.velocescampus.com. Fill in your details, pay the application fee of ₹1,200/- and register for the VSAT exam.
What is VSAT? +
VSAT - Veloces Scholarship and Aptitude Test. It is an online entrance test required as part of the admission process to enrol in B.Tech/B.Sc/BBA programs at Veloces Campus universities.
When is the VSAT exam? +
All students who have aplied for VSAT would be intimated via Email.
Is VSAT Exam an online or an offline examination? +
VSAT Exam is conducted online.
What scholarships are available based on VSAT? +
Based on VSAT performance, students can avail: (1) Upto 100% Scholarship on 1st Year Tuition Fees, (2) Microsoft Women Empowerment Scholarship on Tuition Fee across 4 years (3) Outstanding Achievement Scholarship on Tuition Fee across 4 years.
Is the VSAT application fee refundable? +
No. The VSAT Exam application fee of ₹1,200/- is non-refundable under any circumstances.
What programs does Veloces Campus offer? +
Veloces Campus offers
1. B.Tech CSE in Virtual & Augmented Reality
2. B.Tech CSE in Cyber Forensics & Information Security
3. B.Tech in Robotics & AI
4. B.Tech in AI & Data Science
5. B.Tech CSE in IoT & Cyber Security
6. B.Sc in CS with AI in Immersive Technologies
7. BBA Aviation Management
What technologies are integrated into the curriculum? +
The curriculum integrates Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Internet of Things, Blockchain Technology, Immersive Technologies (AR/VR), and Robotics & Drone Technology.
Where is Stmarys University campus located? +
Stmarys University is located in Hyderabad, Telangana.
What programs does Veloces Campus at Stmarys University offer? +
Veloces Campus at Stmarys University offers specialized programs in rehabilitation sciences, allied health, psychology, and therapy disciplines. The curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with real-world clinical immersion, research, and applied problem-solving.
What is the fee structure at Stmarys University offer? +
The B.Tech program at Stmarys University has a
- Tuition Fee - ₹3,00,000/- per year
- The 1st Year total is ₹3,85,000/- (including Admission Registration Fee of ₹10,000/- and Caution Deposit of ₹50,000/- which is refundable). Hostel fee is ₹1,25,000/- per year for 3-sharing accommodation.
Are there any scholarships available? +
Yes. Students can avail Upto 100% Scholarship* on 1st Year Tuition Fees based on VSAT performance, Microsoft Women Empowerment Scholarship, and Outstanding Achievement Scholarship on tuition fees across all 4 years.
Is hostel facility available? +
Yes. We offer on-campus hostel facility.
What types of hostel rooms are available? +
We offer three sharing rooms.
