# SMRU Backlink, Citation, and Entity Authority Execution Sheet

Goal: Build clean authority for `smru.edu.in` without paid link schemes, spam directories, automated backlinks, or unsupported ranking claims.

## Rules

- Do not buy backlinks.
- Do not use automated backlink creation.
- Do not use exact-match spam anchors like `best university in Hyderabad` repeatedly.
- Do not claim No.1, best, top, first, or only unless backed by official proof.
- Prefer branded, official, and page-relevant anchors.
- Use canonical URLs with trailing slashes when submitting listings.
